Tags NFCs: what is it?

Near-field Communication (NFC) is a set of network protocols that enables communication of electronic devices over a distance of 4cm or less. NFC is based on inductive coupling between two antennas present on NFC devices communicating in one or two directions.

NFC standards are based on existing Radio-Frequency Identification (RFID)

Small demo: writing my WiFi information

  1. Download an application in your android to write RFID tags. In my case, I downloaded the application NFC tools

  2. Enable the Nearest-Field Communication in your phone. Normally it is located in the top bar.

  3. Open the application and search for Ecrire/Write. You have plenty of options. In my case, I found it useful to write a NFC tag with my WiFi password. After getting the information, you should be able to write it easily after being really close to the tag.

  4. Now, just approach your phone to the device.
