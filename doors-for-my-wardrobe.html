Doors for my Wardrobe

Published: mer. 01 mai 2024
By cbernardini

In misc.

tags: woodcraft

During the last summer, I built a wardrobe for my room. It is a pretty big wardrobe of 2m04 by 2h47. I created the whole structure, but I left for the future the installation of the doors.

[wardrobe]

Now, it is time to finish this piece of work and add some sliding doors. The chosen system is pretty easy, we will choose 3 doors of 76.4cm. which sums up ~228cm. In the end, as the wardrobe has 204 it mean there will be 12 cm of overlapping per door. As I don't cut straight, I prefer to avoid manually cutting the doors.

Is it too much?

Is it possible to install two doors of 76.4 and one of 50/60, in order to reduce the overlapping?
