Creating my own coins

Published: lun. 01 juillet 2024
By cbernardini

In misc.

tags: 3dprint

Since I bought my own 3d printer, I have been quite surprised about all the things that we can do with it. In this small experiment, I am going to test printing a very small object. Basically, we will 3d print a coin. This coin will be the same size than a coin 1 euro. To have a small objective, my first goal is to use it in the supermarket just to pick up a trolley."

Iteration #1

In the first iteration, we want to ensure that the size of the coin is exactly the same than 1 euro and also that we can print a small object on top of the coin. In this case will be the Android logo.

List of things validated:

  • Coin has the same size than one euro
  • Coin is only 1g of filament
  • Android logo was successfully printed on top of the image.

Problems found:

  • The printed coin really stick to the 3d printer bed. I made the first scratch on it.
  • I do not know how to clean the 3d printer bed.
  • I made a design in both sides of the coin, but I can only see one of them. The back side was ruined by the 3d printer.
